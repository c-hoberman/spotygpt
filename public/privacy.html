Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2025

[Your App Name] (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website and API at https://spotygpt.onrender.com (the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service, including via the Custom GPT Spotify integration.

1. Information We Collect

  • Spotify Authorization Token
    When you click “Authorize with Spotify,” we never see or store your Spotify password. Instead, we receive a time-limited access token and refresh token directly from Spotify via OAuth. We store these tokens in encrypted form solely to call the Spotify Web API on your behalf.
  • Usage Data
    We log API requests (endpoints called, timestamps) to monitor and debug the Service. These logs do not contain personal listening history or profile data beyond what you explicitly request (e.g., adding a track to a playlist).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • API Calls on Your Behalf
    We use your Spotify access token only to perform actions you’ve requested—such as adding tracks to your playlists. We do not read or store any other data from your account.
  • Analytics & Improvement
    We analyze aggregated, anonymized usage metrics (e.g., number of calls per day) to improve performance and reliability. No personal data is retained in analytics.

3. Data Retention

  • Tokens
    Your Spotify access and refresh tokens persist until you revoke access (in ChatGPT’s sidebar → “Revoke authorization”) or until they expire and refresh fail. After revocation or inactivity, tokens are deleted immediately.
  • Logs
    Request logs are retained up to 30 days and then permanently deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following services:

  • Spotify, Inc. for OAuth authentication and Web API.
  • Render.com for hosting and environment management.
  • OpenAI / ChatGPT for the Custom GPT interface and OAuth callback handling.

5. Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS (HTTPS).
  • Tokens are stored encrypted at rest.
  • Access to production environment variables is limited and audited.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Deletion
    You can revoke our access at any time via Spotify’s Account Settings → Apps, or via the “Revoke authorization” button in ChatGPT’s sidebar. That will delete your tokens from our system.
  • Support
    If you need a copy of any data we hold about you or wish to request deletion of logs, please contact us.
